What is a YouTube Channel Manager?

Before you read this, please, hop on over to another browser tab and search something like: 'how to make my YouTube channel successful' or something like that. See you in a bit...

Welcome back. If you did search something akin to the above, you will have seen the overwhelming amount of information (and, unfortunately, misinformation) that is available to the new YouTube creator.

But it's incredibly difficult and inordinately time-consuming to wade through the muck and myre of the internet to find and implement actually effective strategies for managing and growing a YouTube channel.

This is where I come in...

What I Do

My Channel Management packages are designed to give my clients the least amount of work to do possible. Essentially, I operate on a 'you film it, I handle the rest' basis, giving you the time back to run your business or work your usual 9-5.

I handle the content research and strategy, advising you on what to film; strategic edits to the script; the packaging (thumbnail and title) research and design; the editing, the uploading to YouTube, including all the Description copy and affiliate links (where applicable); and the analytics, which, in turn, drive the research.
