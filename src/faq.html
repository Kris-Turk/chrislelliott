Frequently Asked Questions

How much does YouTube channel management cost?

Channel management costs vary based on your needs and channel size. We offer customized packages starting from $500/month, which includes basic optimization, content strategy, and analytics reporting. Contact us for a personalized quote.

How quickly will I see results?

While every channel is different, most clients see initial improvements in engagement within the first month. Significant subscriber growth typically begins within 3-6 months of implementing our optimization strategies.

Do you handle content creation?

We focus on channel optimization, strategy, and analytics. While we don't create content directly, we provide detailed guidance on content planning, titles, thumbnails, and posting schedules to maximize your existing content's performance.

What metrics do you track?

We monitor key metrics including view duration, click-through rate, subscriber growth, engagement rates, revenue performance, and audience demographics. You'll receive regular reports with actionable insights.

How often will we communicate?

We schedule weekly check-ins and provide monthly performance reports. Additionally, we're available via email for urgent matters and maintain open communication through our preferred project management tools.
