Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about YouTube channel management services

01 How much does YouTube channel management cost?

My Channel Management costs vary and can be tailored to suit your needs and budget. Prices start at £500/month for channel strategy and coaching, and for more comprehensive packages, they are around £2,000/month.

02 How long until I see results?

If we are starting your channel from scratch, I typically advise a 6 month 'bedding in period', where we begin to nurture and learn about your channel and its audience. During this time we will be experimenting with ideas and learning from the ever-expanding data set. If we are developing an existing channel, you can expect to see improvements after 2-3 months.

03 What don't you offer?

I don't create the content for you; you will need to script and film the videos. This is part of building your online brand so it ought to be you creating the content! I do however handle everything else from editing to thumbnails to even uploading and scheduling the videos. My goal is to grow your channel without you having to pull 80 hour weeks.

04 Will I need lots of expensive equipment, like a camera?

NO. If you own a phone that was released this side of 2020, you've already got ALL you need to shoot YouTube videos. Trust me, the quality of phone camera's are more than good enough for YouTube! In fact, even some music videos are now being shot on phones! You also don't need any fancy lighting either!

05 Do you work with smaller channels?

Yes, of course! Whilst I have experience with a larger channel, growing it from 42k to 210k subs, I actually love the excitement of starting a channel from scratch or growing a channel from relative infancy!
